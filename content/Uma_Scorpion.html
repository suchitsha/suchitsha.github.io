UMA Scorpion

Technical Specifications & Details

Overview

A mobile robot engineered for Surveillance purposes, capable of operating in both day and night operations. It offers advanced weapon mount system & mine trigger mechanism for exploding, detecting targets and triggering at a specified point. Its versatility allows operations in all types of terrain. With its robust steering mechanism, the robot offers precise maneuverability and control. It is fully customisable as per requirement of the user.
